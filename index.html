RoVISQ: Reduction of Video Service Quality via Adversarial Attacks on Deep Learning-based Video Compression

Overview of RoVISQ
(a) High-level view of a live video streaming and classification system.
(b) High-level overview of RoVISQ attacks, performed by injecting adversarial perturbations to the front-end video sources.

Attack Methodology
(a) RoVISQ attacks are targeted towards video data generated by front-end sources
(b) We formalize RoVISQ attacks as well-defined optimization problems.
(c) By solving the problem, we can obtain perturbations affecting the R, D, and downstream classification.
